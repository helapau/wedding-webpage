Ceremony

We attended a few dance parties that were held in a space belonging to the Unitarian Church of Prague. We found out about them there and since our impression was good, we decided to ask their spiritual guide Mr. Petr Samojský to guide us through our wedding ceremony.

Dress code

Ceremony as well as the party will be held inside. The ceremony is held in a rather formal, “posh” setting. However after it ends, we will be walking for about 30 minutes, sometimes over cobbles paving. In regards to the April weather, it might be suitable to consider bringing an umbrella and outer clothing. The party is held at a rather informal setting.

Please dress according to your judgement and personal style :)
