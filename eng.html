Welcome to the companion web page to our wedding day. Helena Paulasová & Jan Špaček

Ceremony

Our wedding takes place on Saturday, 5 April 2025. The ceremony will commence at 3 PM.
You can start arriving from 2:30 PM.

The wedding hall is located in the Lobkowicz Palace at Prague Castle, Jiřská 3, 119 00 Praha 1-Hradčany. We recommend to check the map because there are two Lobkowicz palaces in Prague!
Parking near the palace is not possible, we recommend using public transport.

We decided to ask the spiritual guide of the Prague Unitarian community, Mr. Petr Samojský, to conduct the ceremony.
The Unitarians are a free-thinking religious community. We learned about them at a dance event held in their community center. They impressed us primarily with their openness. We are grateful to Mr. Samojský for offering the possibility of performing such a rite of passage, as a wedding is, in a different way compared to the classical view of law and state.

Transfer to the party

After the ceremony ends, there will be free time for transfer to the tram stop Pražský hrad. Link to the route.
At 4:30 PM, our private tram will depart from this stop. Please arrive on time, the tram cannot wait for us.
If you can't make it on time, you can take regular public transport.
The final stop is U Průhonu. From here, it is about 200 m to Město-Moře, where dinner will be waiting for us. The route from U Průhonu to Město-Moře is here.

Since the party takes place in a residential area, we must keep quiet after 10 PM!

What to wear

When choosing your outfit, please remember that we will have to walk around 15 minutes to the tram stop, sometimes over cobbles paving. Both the ceremony and the party will be held indoors. At the party venue, it is necessary to cross a courtyard with fine gravel.

The main part of the program is a swing dance block. Comfortable shoes are generally suitable for swing dancing, preferably with a lower heel.

Please dress according to your judgement and personal style :)
